About Ragdoll Archers

Ragdoll Archers is an exciting physics-based archery game where you control a ragdoll character armed with a bow and arrow. Your mission is to defeat opponents by precisely aiming and shooting arrows at them before they hit you.

With realistic physics simulations, each movement and shot creates a unique experience. The game features intuitive controls, exciting levels, and challenging opponents that will test your archery skills.

Perfect for quick gaming sessions, Ragdoll Archers offers endless fun with its simple yet addictive gameplay. Can you become the ultimate archer?

How to Play

Controls

  • Use your mouse to aim the bow
  • Click and hold to charge your shot
  • Release to fire an arrow
  • Use A and D keys (or arrow keys) to move left and right
  • Press Space to jump

Tips & Tricks

  • Aim slightly above your target to account for arrow drop
  • Fully charged shots deal more damage
  • Keep moving to avoid enemy arrows
  • Time your jumps to dodge incoming attacks
  • Try to hit enemy head for critical damage
